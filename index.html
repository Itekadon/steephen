About Us!

Hello and Welcome to Dads Unity Hub.....(DUH), a community dedicated to providing support and resources to single fathers who are going through the challenging journey of raising children alone after the loss of a partner.

Our platform enables single fathers to connect with each other, share experiences, find guidance, and easy access to a bunch of information they need.

We also offer a variety of resources to help you navigate the path of single parenting, which include the following:

  • Expert Advice - information on legal rights, child custody, and support services available to single fathers in South Africa
  • Woman's Gallery - to honor memories of your partners who have since passed
  • Men's Gallery - to celebrate moments you and your children share
  • Blog & Testimonials - to share personal stories from other dads and their children
  • Dating Resources - to assist you with finding companionship or a partner in crime when you are ready
  • Nanny's Profiles - to help you find a reliable child minder, housekeeper or reputable day cares.

At DUH , We believe that support should be accessible to everyone, anytime, anywhere. So, don't hesitate ! Sign Up and create your account right away.
